Tax Implications of Phoenix payroll issues

The Canada Revenue Agency and a PSAC member have reached a settlement regarding the taxability of the lump-sum payment received by PSAC members under the 2020 Phoenix pay system damages agreement. Under the terms of the settlement, the portion for stress and damages (up to $1,500) is non-taxable. To benefit from the settlement, you must have filed a notice of objection disputing the taxation of this amount on April 30, 2024 or before.
